# Privacy Policy

**Symptom Tracker**
Last updated: March 31, 2026

## Overview

Symptom Tracker is a personal health diary app. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built this app. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** All symptom entries, health information, and app preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers. We do not have servers that receive your data.

## What Data the App Stores Locally

The app stores the following information exclusively on your device:

- Symptom names, categories, and severity ratings
- Duration, body location, and notes
- Triggers and relief actions
- App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up.

## Google Drive Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable Google Drive backup:

- A backup file is stored in your own Google Drive account in a protected app-specific folder
- Only you and this app can access that file — no other apps or users can see it
- We never have access to your Google account or your backup data
- You can delete the backup at any time from your Google Drive
- The app requests only the minimum `drive.appdata` permission, which limits access to a hidden app-specific folder

## Third-Party Services

The app does not include:

- Analytics or tracking SDKs
- Advertising frameworks
- Crash reporting that transmits personal data
- Any service that collects or processes your health information

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Period.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- Uninstalling the app deletes all local data
- You can clear app data from Android Settings > Apps > Symptom Tracker > Clear Data
- Google Drive backups can be deleted from your Google Drive account

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app stores data only on the user's device.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected in the app's repository and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
